Resume Tips for Success

Use Simple Formatting

Keep your resume clean and simple to ensure it’s ATS-friendly. Avoid headers, footers, and complex graphics that might confuse applicant tracking systems.

Incorporate Keywords

Tailor your resume with keywords from the job description. This increases your chances of passing ATS filters and getting noticed by recruiters.

Focus on Achievements

Highlight measurable achievements rather than just listing duties. Use numbers to show impact, like “Increased sales by 20%” or “Managed a team of 10.”

Keep It Concise

Aim for a one-page resume if you have less than 10 years of experience. Be concise and relevant, focusing on your most recent and impactful roles.
